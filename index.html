About Me

My journey in data science

My data journey began unexpectedly in grad school when a professor challenged us to "Find some data to answer questions you have." What started as an assignment quickly became a passion as I discovered I could solve real problems through data analysis.

I get genuine satisfaction from creating visualizations that make complex information immediately understandable. There's something rewarding about watching someone have that "aha moment" when they see their data presented in a way that finally makes sense.

My background combines economics theory with practical data skills, but I'm not tied to any specific industry—I enjoy the challenge of learning new business domains and applying analytical thinking to different problems.

Before diving into data, I played basketball at the University of La Verne and later competed in collegiate esports at Boise State, where our team won the Mountain West region and competed at a LAN tournament in Georgia. When I'm not working with data, you'll find me at the gym, playing piano, gaming with friends, or being commanded around by my cat Stella, who's convinced that random sprints around the apartment at 1 AM are essential to our household operations.
